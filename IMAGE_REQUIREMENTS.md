# Image Files Needed for SEO

Please add the following images to the `/public` directory for complete SEO optimization:

## Required Images:

1. **favicon.ico** (32x32px) - Main favicon
2. **favicon-192.png** (192x192px) - PWA icon
3. **favicon-512.png** (512x512px) - PWA icon  
4. **og-image.jpg** (1200x630px) - Open Graph image for social media sharing
5. **apple-touch-icon.png** (180x180px) - iOS home screen icon

## Design Recommendations:

- Use your initials "SB" or a professional headshot
- Brand colors: Blue (#3b82f6) and dark/light theme compatible
- Ensure images are crisp and professional
- Include your name or professional title in the og-image

## Tools for Creation:

- Canva, Figma, or Adobe Creative Suite
- Online favicon generators
- Image optimization tools like TinyPNG

Once created, these images will complete your SEO setup.
